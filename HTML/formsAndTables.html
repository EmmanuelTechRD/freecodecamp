What Is Client-Side Form Validation in HTML Forms, and What Are Some Examples?

When a user fills up information in a form, it is important to verify that they are providing all the necessary information in the correct format. This is why form validation is important; it can be enforced both on the front-end layout, on the interface the user interacts with on their browser, as well as on the back-end or server side for added security. One example of form validation is the required attribute with input elements. This attribute specifies that the user needs to fill up an specific form field, before they are able to send the form.
